A Magical Friendship

Once upon a time, in a big, green forest, there was a little rabbit named Benny. Benny was small and fluffy, and he loved to hop around the forest, eating delicious carrots.

One sunny day, while Benny was nibbling on a carrot, he heard a loud roar. It was Teo, the friendly tiger. Teo was big and had orange stripes all over his body, but he was very kind.

Benny was a bit scared at first, but Teo just wanted to be friends. He said, "Hi, I'm Teo, the tiger. What's your name?"

Benny replied, "I'm Benny, the rabbit. Nice to meet you."

From that day on, Benny and Teo became the best of friends. They played games together, shared stories, and even had picnics in the forest. Teo promised to protect Benny from any dangers in the forest.

And so, in the big, green forest, a little rabbit and a friendly tiger showed that even the most different friends could have the most fun adventures together. They proved that friendship can be found in unexpected places.

The end.
